# Privacy Policy — Somnio

**Version:** 1.0
**Effective date:** June 2026
**Last updated:** June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Somnio ("we", "our", "the app") handles your information. Somnio is a sleep tracker that records and analyzes your sleep entirely on your iPhone. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

---

## TL;DR — The short version

* Somnio runs **100% on your device**. No cloud, no servers, no accounts.
* We **do not collect, store, or transmit** any personal data outside your iPhone.
* We **do not track you**. There are no analytics SDKs, no advertising identifiers, and no third-party services collecting user data.
* We **do not sell or share** your data with anyone — because we never have it.
* You can **delete everything** at any time from Settings → Delete All Data.

## 2. What data Somnio processes

All processing happens **locally on your iPhone**. We never receive a copy.

| Data type                                             | Source                                               | Where it's stored               | Used for                        |
| ----------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------- | ------------------------------- |
| Audio recordings (segments of snoring, talking, etc.) | iPhone microphone                                    | App sandbox on this iPhone only | Categorizing your sleep sounds  |
| Motion data (accelerometer magnitude)                 | iPhone motion sensors                                | App sandbox on this iPhone only | Detecting movement during sleep |
| Sleep schedule (bedtime, wake-up time)                | Entered by you                                       | App sandbox on this iPhone only | Configuring alarms              |
| Alarm templates (sound, volume, snooze)               | Configured by you                                    | App sandbox on this iPhone only | Triggering alarms               |
| Optional HealthKit sleep data                         | Apple Health / HealthKit, with your explicit consent | Apple Health (managed by iOS)   | Enriching sleep analysis        |

---

## 3. Permissions Somnio asks for

Somnio requests the following iOS permissions. Each can be revoked at any time in **iPhone Settings → Somnio**.

### Microphone

Required to record sleep sounds. Recordings remain on your iPhone and are stored only within the app's sandbox.

### Motion & Fitness

Required to detect movement patterns during sleep and improve sleep analysis.

### Apple Health / HealthKit (Optional)

Only requested if you choose to read or write sleep-related data through HealthKit. The app remains functional if permission is denied.

### Notifications

Required for alarms and sleep reminders to work reliably.

## 4. What we do NOT collect

Somnio does **not** collect:

* ❌ Account information
* ❌ Email addresses
* ❌ Passwords
* ❌ Location data
* ❌ Contacts
* ❌ Calendar information
* ❌ Photos
* ❌ Files outside the app sandbox
* ❌ Analytics events
* ❌ Advertising identifiers (IDFA)
* ❌ Fingerprinting data
* ❌ Behavioral tracking information
* ❌ Audio recordings stored on remote servers
* ❌ Crash reports sent to third parties

Apple may collect certain aggregated diagnostics or analytics information if you enable those features in iOS. Somnio does not receive or access that information.

---

## 5. Network communication

Somnio does not operate any backend servers.

The app functions entirely on-device and does not upload audio recordings, motion data, sleep statistics, alarm settings, or user preferences to any remote infrastructure.

## 7. How long Somnio keeps your data

Your data remains on your device for as long as you choose.

You have full control over retention:

* Delete individual recordings from the recordings list.
* Use **Settings → Delete All Data** to permanently erase all recordings, sleep statistics, alarm templates, and preferences.
* Uninstalling the app removes the app sandbox and all associated data according to iOS behavior.

## 8. Children's privacy

Somnio is rated 4+ on the App Store but is intended for individuals who want to monitor and improve their sleep.

Because Somnio does not collect personal information and does not transmit data off-device, we do not knowingly collect information from children or adults of any age.

## 9. Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Because all data remains on your device, you exercise your rights directly through the app.

### Right of Access

All data stored by Somnio is visible directly on your device.

### Right to Deletion

You may delete your data at any time through Settings → Delete All Data or by uninstalling the app.

### Right to Portability

You may export or share recordings using standard iOS sharing functionality.

### Right to Rectification

You may edit alarm templates, schedules, and preferences at any time.

### Right to Object or Opt Out

You may stop using the app and remove all data by uninstalling it.

If you believe Somnio has processed data outside your device, please contact us at **[support@somnio.app](mailto:support@somnio.app)**.
